Projects

  • Equipment Load Calculation App (Japan)(iOS)

    Enterprise App

    With the train being Japan's number one mode of transportation, it is just fitting enough that this mode of transportation has one of the best maintenance procedures found today. And this application would be part of that process to better help the ones maintaining these incredibale machines. With the app, maintenance teams would be able to draw up a sketch of a beam supplying electricity to the trains on the ipad with accurate measurements and material types this then caclulates the current stress and load of a certain part depending on the wind gust and direction. This then gives them an immediate result which can be shared to other devices which will give them a much faster response rate to special situations.

  • Railway Surveying App (Japan)(iOS/Bluetooth/C++)

    Enterprise App

    This app is specially designed to support the surveying team on the field. Since the previous process of getting the distance between the railway and the trolley line was manually calculated and has been considered dangerous since surveying team members would be standing in the railway with a measurement device, this application was created to allow them to measure from a distance. This app will be connected to laser measuring device and a wifi-enabled camera to allow them to measure from a safer distance. Manipulating the device is also done within the application since it is created with a custom hardware that will control the device’s rotation. Thus, giving the surveyor full control of the device from a far while maintaining accurate measurements.

  • Railway Detection App (Japan)(iOS/OpenCV)

    Enterprise App

    This system aims to help field surveyors to picture out how to position electric poles along the railway by analyzing a picture taken on the field. The app detects and calculates valid distance from the railway to place the pole. This is in turn will help the main office to analyse and decide constructions along the railway.
